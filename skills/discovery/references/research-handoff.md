# Research Handoff

Use this when discovery needs outside evidence without giving up PM ownership.

## Invoke when

- The client asks for reference products, comparisons, or "what do others use?"
- A requirement depends on pricing, licensing, compliance, region, or platform availability
- The PM needs grounded choices for login, payments, chat, search, maps, analytics, CMS, or vendors
- A vague preference needs real-world examples before the next client question

## PM -> Researcher

- Decision to unblock
- Project constraints
- Client language to mirror
- Output length and urgency

## Researcher -> PM

- 2-4 realistic options
- Default recommendation
- Evidence / source list
- Open unknowns
- PM converts the brief into the next client question and updates `spec.md`

## Researcher -> CTO

- Carry forward any brief that changes stack, vendor, integration, or compliance assumptions
- Flag constraints the CTO must preserve during design

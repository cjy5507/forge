# Research Integration

Use this when design depends on external options, not just framework docs.

## Invoke when

- Discovery handed off research that affects stack, vendor, or compliance choices
- CTO must choose between viable frameworks, packages, services, or hosting options
- Pricing, licensing, region, support, or migration risk changes the design
- Market examples are needed before locking an architecture direction

## Researcher -> CTO

- Recommendation with 2-4 realistic options
- Rejected options and why
- Integration constraints and compatibility notes
- Evidence / source list
- Open risks the CTO must either accept or avoid

## Decision rule

- Researcher informs the choice
- CTO makes the architecture call
- CTO still verifies exact framework behavior via `context7`
